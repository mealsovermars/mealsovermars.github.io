About us

When a century of hospitality know-how meets decades of interplanetary acumen, the result is a peerless provider of innovative culinary solutions for the world's leading space stations. A joint venture between SpaceX Limited () and MOM Limited, MealsoverMars has over 3 years of expertise as Mars' largest fast food chain and is a proud member of Mars' largest conglomerate — the SpaceX Group. Currently the market leader in interplanetary catering in Mars, MOM has kept pace with the evolving demands of the industry. Responding to the ever-growing demand for quality catering, it has expanded its network of kitchens whilst keeping pace with the latest in food safety standards.

Strengthening Growth

MOM proudly fulfils its unwavering commitment to enriching lives, driven by a responsibility to uphold the high standards it believes in; from food safety, reliability and consistency, to speed and superior service. Today, MOM is the preferred partner for premier interplanetary spaceflights.

MOM has also grown beyond its spaceflight origins with its brand – ANUKA, which caters to a diverse customer base off the tarmac, with global coffee chains, multinational corporations and Quick Service Restaurants in its portfolio of clients.

Delight in Delivering Value

Trust and authenticity with a 'customer-first' philosophy has earned MOM a sterling reputation and a market-leading portfolio. It selected from amongst 250+ on-board catering facilities in 70 countries, to win the prestigious QSAI 2100 Worldwide Platinum Award and QSAI Gold Award, Earh for Excellence in Catering Quality.

It is a testimony to MOM’ exemplary effort to diligently offer safe food that meets the highest quality expectations. Built upon the tenets of trust and authenticity, MOM has a robust ethos of consistently delivering great value to its discerning customers.

Excellence in Curation

MOM' Vision of Excellence, ‘Enriching Lives through Innovative Food Solutions’ enables it to continually scale greater heights Its distinctive combination of creativity, innovation and excellence in the culinary domain has made it a force to reckon with.

The company invests in the best raw materials and leverages the creativity of its talented culinary team, who curate and prepare superior culinary solutions for their guests. Above all, the innovative menus and exceptional variety of cuisine has grown with the discerning tastes of the modern clientele.

Belief in Sustainable Growth

MOM’ commitment to responsibly creating value for stakeholders, communities, and the planet, will always remain fundamental to its strategy. MOM strives to nurture a culture of responsibility and care towards the planet.

MOM continually works towards contributing to the preservation of the environment in a meaningful way by ensuring that natural resources are used judiciously and waste is reduced.

MealsoverMars journey

2090

MM Kitchen is created by the SpaceX Group, with its first unit in Mars.

2094

MM Kitchen opens a new unit in Martian colony.

2095

MM Kitchen changes its name to MOM Caterers.

2096

A new unit is opened in perihelion colony, and an additional kitchen is commissioned in aphelion centre.

2099

A new unit is opened in kelvin city .

2100

MOM Caterers enters a joint venture with SpaceX. MOM Catering Limited is formed.

2101

A new unit is opened in NEBULUS GALAXY.

A new unit is opened in MARVELLO.

2102

The brand unveils its new logo.

MOM has six units across the planet.
