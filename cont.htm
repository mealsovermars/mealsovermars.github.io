OPERATIONAL EXCELLENCE

TajSATS strives to drive operational excellence by always focusing on customer needs, enabling and empowering its workforce to ensure that principles of excellence are ingrained in everyday activities, and continually improving processes.

To build capabilities and support partners, TajSATS has invested in modern, eco-efficient and state-of-the art equipment. GPS tracking programs, X-Ray machines at receiving area of raw materials, air showers, solar panels, Crawford doors at the loading bays, etc, are some of the many initiatives undertaken to ensure operational excellence. The kitchens have the most modern equipment, such as egg breaking machines, rotary ovens and blast freezers, among others.

TajSATS believes in operating in the most environmentally responsible manner and complying with locally-applicable environment regulations and standards. It is dedicated to adherence to policies relating to energy and waste management and minimisation. There is a waste composting plant in every unit of TajSATS.

The workforce is trained to pay meticulous attention to detail, right from receiving goods at the shop floor to the final delivery to the aircraft. The partners are handled by trained and experienced Duty Managers who ensure safety, precision and receptiveness. TajSATS believes that every employee at each level of the organisation is doing a job that brings value to the customer and is therefore on the critical path to success.

SERVICE INTEGRITY

TajSATS' associates work relentlessly across the dimensions that influence exceptional customer service with the seamless delivery of over 65,000 meals a month. There is complete adherence to the set governance guidelines, policies and best practices.

From receiving raw materials, storage, cooking to blast chilling, portioning and tray-setting to the final dispatch, each is done with clockwork precision.

TajSATS always goes the extra mile to provide innovative, efficient and reliable service to its customers, striving to achieve excellence in every critical control point so that the customer experiences the best meal and service.
